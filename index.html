About me

I am a postdoctoral researcher in the department of Computer Science at Purdue University under the supervision of Prof. Xiangyu Zhang. My research interests span the areas of systems security and software engineering, specifically focusing on mobile and smart devices security.

Recent News:
  • I will be joining the Cheriton School of Computer Science at the University of Waterloo as an assistant professor beginning January 2020!

  • I am serving on the program committee of EuroSys 2019, consider submitting your awesome work!
  • I am serving on the program committee of ACSAC 2019, please submit your great work!
